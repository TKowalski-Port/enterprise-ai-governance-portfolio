## **Purpose**  
Records the governance decision for the Enterprise AI Assistant use case, including its rationale, conditions, and next actions.

## **Scope**

Applies to the governance decision for the Enterprise AI Assistant use case and the conditions required before approval may be reconsidered.

## **Executive Decision**

> **The Enterprise AI Assistant use case is deferred pending implementation and validation of required governance controls. Approval may be reconsidered once the controls are operating as intended, the risk and human-impact reassessment is complete, target residual risk is confirmed as Medium, and defined risk tolerance and control performance targets are met.**

## **Decision Basis**

- The use case presents a **High inherent risk**.

- Required governance controls have not yet been fully implemented and validated.

- Human review is required before AI-generated content may be approved or published.

- Current exposure includes unauthorized AI use, inaccurate content, changes to approved control intent, reliance on non-authoritative output, and bypass of document governance requirements.

## **Conditions for Approval**

Approval may be reconsidered when:

- AI-C01 through AI-C05 are implemented and operating as intended.

- Human review and validation are embedded before approval or publication.

- Monitoring, exception, and escalation processes are operational.

- Target residual risk is confirmed as **Medium** and all defined risk tolerance and control performance targets are met.

## **Decision Authority**

**Executive Leadership**

The AI Governance Committee provides governance review and recommendation. GRC provides risk oversight.

Because current risk exceeds appetite, the deferral is reported to the Board for awareness through the next scheduled AI risk update.

## **Required Next Actions**

1\. Implement the selected governance controls.

2\. Validate control design and operation.

3\. Complete the risk and human-impact reassessment.

4\. Confirm target residual risk and compliance with the defined risk tolerance and control performance targets.

5\. Return the use case for final approval.

## **Reassessment Triggers**

The decision must be reassessed when there is:

- a material change to the use case, platform, data, or integration;

- a control failure, incident, or unauthorized use;

- a change in legal, regulatory, or internal requirements;

- a change in human-impact or consequential-use considerations;

- evidence that current or residual risk exceeds approved tolerance.

## **Final Disposition**

**Decision State:** Deferred  
**Risk Response:** Mitigate  
**Target Residual Risk:** Medium  
**Approval Status:** Pending control implementation, validation, reassessment, and final approval.
