## **Purpose**

Summarizes the current risk, control status, business impact, and governance decision for the Enterprise AI Assistant use case.

## **Scope**  
Covers the Enterprise AI Assistant use case, associated governance controls, risk scenarios, decision status, and required actions over the 12-month assessment period.

## **Observed Issue**

Unapproved use of a public AI service involving nonpublic policy content was identified, investigated, and added to the AI inventory. A governed enterprise AI process for policy development was initiated. No confirmed exposure of restricted or personal data was identified.

## **Management Attention**

| **Priority Matter** | **Business Exposure** | **Required Action** | **Owner** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Unapproved AI use involving nonpublic policy content | Loss of control over sensitive information; potential legal, security, privacy, and reputational harm | Complete implementation and validation of AI-C01 through AI-C05 | Control Owners |
| Current risk (High) is outside tolerance | Governance, compliance, and control exposure; reported to the Board for awareness | Validate required controls and complete reassessment before reconsideration | GRC |
| Human review not yet operational | Risk of unvalidated or unauthorized content entering controlled documents | Embed independent human review and validation before approval or publication | Document or Policy Owner |

| **Impact Area** | **Potential Effect** |

| Digital Trust | Reduced confidence in Enterprise Company's ability to govern AI and protect information |
| Legal and Regulatory | Failure to meet internal requirements or applicable obligations |
| Operations | Defective or unauthorized content entering controlled business processes |

## **Control Status**

| **Control** | **Status** | **Purpose** |

| AI-C01 — AI Governance Policy | 🔴 Not Yet Validated | Establishes authorized use, responsibilities, and governance requirements |
| AI-C02 — AI Inventory and Authorization | 🔴 Not Yet Validated | Reduces unknown and unapproved AI use |
| AI-C03 — Human Review and Validation | 🔴 Not Yet Validated | Reduces inaccurate or inappropriate content entering controlled documents |
| AI-C04 — Document Change Governance | 🔴 Not Yet Validated | Protects approved control intent and document integrity |
| AI-C05 — Monitoring and Exception Management | 🔴 Not Yet Validated | Identifies emerging risk and enables reassessment, escalation, or retirement |

## **Executive Interpretation**

- Current risk is High because AI use and generated content are not yet fully governed.
- Three of five risk scenarios (AI-01, AI-03, AI-05) are individually rated High; none of the five required controls are yet validated.
- The principal business exposures affect reputation, financial performance, legal obligations, operations, and digital trust.
- The use case remains deferred until required controls are implemented and validated, and is reported to the Board for awareness because current risk is High and outside tolerance.
- Target state is Medium residual risk, achieved only when required controls are validated, tolerance and control performance targets are met, and human review and approval are retained for controlled documents.
