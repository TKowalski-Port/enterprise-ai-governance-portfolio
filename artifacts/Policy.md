## **Purpose**  
Establish clear requirements for the responsible governance, oversight, and use of AI systems and tools across the organization.

## **Scope**

Applies to all personnel who develop, procure, deploy, use, or manage AI systems and tools on behalf of the organization.

## **Definitions**

Terms used in this policy are defined in the Enterprise Company Glossary, maintained separately and not included in this portfolio.

## **Roles and Responsibilities**

**Board**  
Oversight of material AI risk.   

**Executive Leadership**  
Accountability for AI governance and risk. 

**AI Governance Committee**  
Governance decisions and oversight.  

**Business and Technology Owners**   
Own approved AI use cases and implement required governance and controls.                                                                                    

**GRC**  
Governance, risk, and compliance oversight.  

**Legal, Privacy, and Information Security**  
Review and guidance within their areas of responsibility.                                                          

**Internal Audit**  
Independent assessment of AI governance and control effectiveness.

**Document or Policy Owner**  
Accountable for reviewing and approving AI-assisted changes to documents they own.

**Personnel**  
Follow this policy and report suspected violations or risks.                                                               

## **Policy**

<div style="margin-left: 2rem;">

1. &nbsp; &nbsp; AI systems and tools must support an authorized business purpose.

2. &nbsp; &nbsp; AI systems and tools must comply with applicable laws, regulations, and organizational requirements.

3. &nbsp; &nbsp; AI-related risks must be assessed before use, following material change, and at defined periodic intervals.

4. &nbsp; &nbsp; Humans must remain accountable for AI-informed decisions and actions.

5. &nbsp; &nbsp; AI systems and tools must be used consistently with company values, as defined in the Code of Conduct and Ethics policies.

6. &nbsp; &nbsp; AI systems and tools must follow the approved lifecycle from intake through retirement, including required human review and approval.

</div>
 
## **Compliance**

Violations may result in access restrictions, removal of the AI system or tool, or other corrective action.

## **Exceptions**

Exceptions must follow the organization's [Exceptions and Escalation Process](Exceptions-and-Escalation.md).
