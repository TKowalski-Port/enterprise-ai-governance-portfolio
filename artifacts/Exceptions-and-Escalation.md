## **Purpose**  
Defines how requests to deviate from AI requirements are reviewed, decided, monitored, escalated, and closed. 

## **Scope**  
Applies to requests to deviate from AI requirements and matters that exceed established authority or risk tolerance.

## **Process**

Exception Identified → Risk Review → Risk Appetite Check → Decision 
 
- **Approved** → Monitor → Close
- **Escalated** → Return to Risk Review at appropriate authority level
- **Denied** → Close 

## **Exception Decision Outcomes**

- **Approved:** May proceed with defined conditions, an owner, monitoring, and an expiration date.
- **Escalated:** Exceeds risk tolerance or decision authority, involves legal, privacy, security, or human impact, or lacks required controls.
- **Denied:** May not proceed; requester must comply with the existing requirement.


## **Human Review**

A human must review any matter rated Major or higher for Impact, or High or higher for Human Impact, before a final decision.

## **Boundary**

This process does not replace incident response, legal review, disciplinary action, or the AI governance workflow.
