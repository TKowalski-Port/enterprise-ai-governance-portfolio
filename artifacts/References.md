# **Governance Reference**

This reference defines the risk, decision, and escalation criteria used across the Enterprise Company AI governance portfolio.


## **Likelihood Ratings**
**Assessment Period:** 12 months.

- **Rare (1):** Exceptional circumstances; not expected during the assessment period.

- **Unlikely (2):** Could occur, but there is limited evidence or opportunity.

- **Possible (3):** Could reasonably occur during the assessment period.

- **Likely (4):** Expected to occur or supported by observed activity or recurring conditions.

- **Almost Certain (5):** Expected to occur repeatedly or is already occurring frequently.

## **Impact Ratings**

- **Minor (1):** Limited and readily reversible effect with routine remediation.

- **Moderate (2):** Meaningful effect; requires corrective action.

- **Major (3):** Material consequence requiring management attention.

- **Severe (4):** Enterprise-level or sustained consequence requiring immediate executive action.

- **Catastrophic (5):** Existential or irreversible consequences to the organization.

## **Risk Calculation & Ratings**

Risk score = Likelihood value × Impact value.

- **Low 1–3:** Limited exposure requiring routine management.

- **Medium 4–7:** Meaningful exposure requiring defined controls and oversight.

- **High 8–14:** Significant exposure requiring treatment and management attention.

- **Critical 15–25:** Severe exposure requiring immediate action and executive escalation.

Overall inherent risk reflects the highest individual risk-scenario rating within the assessment.

## **Decision States**

- **Approved:** The use case may proceed subject to defined controls and monitoring.

- **Deferred:** Additional information, remediation, or control implementation is required before approval.

- **Rejected:** The use case must not proceed.

## **Risk Appetite**

No appetite for:  
- Unauthorized disclosure of nonpublic information
- Publication of AI-generated controlled content without human approval

**Tolerance**  
0 for both.  

**Targets**  
100% documented approval on controlled content; 100% AI use case registration.  

**Medium Residual Risk**  
Acceptable only if controls are validated and no escalation criterion is met.

**High/Critical Residual Risk**  
Never acceptable.  


## **Risk Responses**

- **Mitigate:** Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact.

- **Accept:** Formally acknowledge and retain risk within approved appetite.

- **Avoid:** Stop or prohibit the activity creating the risk.

- **Transfer:** Shift defined financial or operational exposure to another party.

## **Human-Impact Ratings**

- **Low:** Minimal effect on individuals or groups.

- **Medium:** Indirect or contained effect; requires review and safeguards.

- **High:** Significant effect; requires enhanced review and executive oversight.

- **Critical:** Consequential effect; must not proceed without formal authorization.

## **Escalation Criteria**

Escalation is required when:

- residual risk exceeds approved tolerance;

- material legal, privacy, security, or human impact exists;

- required controls cannot be implemented;

- the matter exceeds the current decision-maker’s authority.
