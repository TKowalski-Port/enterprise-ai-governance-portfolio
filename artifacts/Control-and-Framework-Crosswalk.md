## **Mapping Approach**

- Each control is mapped to the NIST outcomes that most directly support its governance or risk-management purpose.

- Mappings indicate alignment only and do not establish full equivalence, implementation, conformance, or certification.

- Framework descriptions are based on the official NIST publications.

Mapping Note: AI-C03 aligns with the output-interpretation and responsible-use elements of NIST AI RMF MEASURE 2.9. It does not represent independent validation of the underlying AI model.
