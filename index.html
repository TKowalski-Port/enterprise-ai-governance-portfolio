About

Tom Kowalski's career spans public relations, media intelligence, information security, and enterprise risk. Across those disciplines, he noticed a consistent gap: technical, operational, and reputational risks are assessed in isolation. AI now accelerates all three, and compresses the time leaders have to respond. AI governance closes that gap, bringing all three together so leaders can decide quickly when the stakes are highest. He is a Certified Cybersecurity Strategist through Georgetown University and holds a BS in Marketing from Central Connecticut State University.

How I Help

I help organizations build practical strategies, with clear ownership, accountability, and oversight, enabling risk-informed decisions that drive innovation and adoption without exposing the organization to unnecessary risk.
