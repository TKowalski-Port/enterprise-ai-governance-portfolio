## **Overview**  
Enterprise Company is a fictional diversified organization created solely for portfolio demonstration. The portfolio shows the governance judgment, risk analysis, decision structures, controls, and oversight mechanisms used to manage AI adoption across the enterprise.

## **Purpose**  
Demonstrates practical AI governance through risk assessment, decision-making, controls, oversight, and accountability.


## **Scope**

This portfolio illustrates a practical AI governance approach for professional evaluation. It includes governance structure, risk analysis, policy, workflow, selected control mappings, executive reporting, and decision documentation.

It does not represent a full technical implementation, certified assessment, or complete enterprise control library.

Full reuse terms are set out in [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md).

## **Why AI Governance**  

AI governance establishes clear accountability, decision authority, and risk oversight as AI adoption expands.

## **The Governance Challenge**

Enterprise Company is expanding its use of artificial intelligence across business functions. AI adoption is occurring faster than the organization's governance processes, creating inconsistent approvals, unclear accountability, unmanaged risk, and limited executive visibility.

The governance objective is to establish a practical, risk-based system that:

- defines authority and accountability;

- evaluates AI risks before deployment;

- establishes mandatory governance requirements;

- operationalizes review, approval, monitoring, and escalation;

- provides leadership with meaningful oversight.
